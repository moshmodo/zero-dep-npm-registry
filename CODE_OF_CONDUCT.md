# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background or characteristics. We strive to foster an open, welcoming, and healthy environment.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include empathy, kindness, respecting differing views, constructive feedback, and community-focused actions.
Unacceptable behaviors include harassment, sexualized language, trolling, and sharing private info without permission.

## Enforcement

Project leaders will address inappropriate behavior and have the authority to remove or reject contributions.
Issues can be reported to [the project leader](mailto:brunosimard.z@gmail.com).

## Enforcement Guidelines

Consequences follow a structure based on community impact:
1.  **Correction:** Private warning for unprofessional behavior.
2.  **Warning:** Temporary restriction of interactions.
3.  **Temporary Ban:** Serious violation, resulting in a temporary ban.
4.  **Permanent Ban:** Continued, serious, or pattern-based violations.
